Hello world — why I started this blog.

Welcome to my corner of the internet. I built this site to have one place for the things I'm working on, the things I'm learning, and the occasional thought I want to keep around longer than a tweet.

What you'll find here

Mostly short posts about projects, notes on what I'm reading, and write-ups of things I figure out along the way. I'd rather post something useful and short than wait for a perfect long-form essay that never ships.

How it's built

This site is plain HTML and CSS — no framework, no build step. It's hosted on GitHub Pages with a custom domain. The whole source is open at github.com/ayoubg1 if you want to poke around.

Thanks for stopping by — more soon.
